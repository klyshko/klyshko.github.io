The 2022 **Graduate Research Colloquium (GRC)** at UTM was held **in person** in collaboration with the **Vice-Dean Graduate** office, the **UTM Library**, and [**UTM AGS (UTMAGS)**](https://utmags.sa.utoronto.ca/). The official [announcement (April 11, 2022)](https://utmags.sa.utoronto.ca/2022/04/11/2022-graduate-research-colloquium-grc/) gives the format: **Tuesday, May 3** (**3:00–5:00 p.m.**) for **poster** presentations, and **Wednesday, May 4** (**2:00–4:00 p.m.**) for oral talks, with a guest talk by Ron Deibert (Citizen Lab) on May 4 at 4:00 p.m. Registration closed **April 22, 2022**.

I presented a **poster** on **LAWS: Local alignment for water sites** in protein simulations during the **May 3** poster block—the **same** poster I later took to the [joint BSC / IUPAB 2022 meeting in Ottawa](/talks/2022-05-26-bsc-poster/). At GRC, the poster received a **best poster** award.
