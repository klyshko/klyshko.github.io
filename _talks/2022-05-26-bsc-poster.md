The [7th annual meeting of the Biophysical Society of Canada](https://event.fourwaves.com/jointbsc2022iupab) was held in person at the **University of Ottawa** (May 25–27, 2022), alongside an **IUPAB** focused meeting on the biophysics of ligand-gated ion channels (May 23–25, 2022).

I presented a poster on **LAWS: Local alignment for water sites** in protein simulations; it was the **same** work I had shown a few weeks earlier at [UTM GRC 2022](/talks/2022-05-03-utm-grc-poster/), where it received a **best poster** award. More on the BSC: [Biophysical Society of Canada](https://biophysicalsociety.ca/), [IUPAB joint meeting context](https://iupab.org/wp-content/uploads/sites/7/2022/12/Newsletter81.pdf).
