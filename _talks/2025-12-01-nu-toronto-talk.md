Fernandes, PhD** with **Northeastern University Toronto** students, on how **generative AI** is changing **modern drug discovery**: de novo binders and protein therapeutics, where models help and where they break, and how the most useful work combines **computational tools with experiments, regulation, and domain expertise** (industry perspective from **Fable Therapeutics**).
