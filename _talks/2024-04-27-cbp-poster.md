The [Chemical Biophysics Symposium](https://www.chembiophys.ca/) is a graduate-student–organized meeting at the University of Toronto. The **21st** symposium took place in **2024** (see [past symposia](https://www.chembiophys.ca/past-symposia)); a widely circulated announcement for the 2024 edition listed **April 26–28, 2024** at the University of Toronto.

I presented a poster on computational and molecular work connected to our protein / water dynamics and crystallography-related studies.
