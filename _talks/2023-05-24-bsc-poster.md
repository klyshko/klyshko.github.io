The [8th annual meeting of the Biophysical Society of Canada](https://biophysicalsociety.ca/) took place **May 23–26, 2023** on the campus of the **University of Calgary**.

I presented a poster on **LAWS: Local alignment for water sites** and ordered water in simulations. The poster was recognized with a **best poster** award. [Program PDF (BSC 2023)](https://biophysicalsociety.ca/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/BSC2023_ProgramFinal.pdf).
