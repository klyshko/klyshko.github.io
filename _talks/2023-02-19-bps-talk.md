The [Biophysical Society 67th Annual Meeting](https://www.biophysics.org/2023meeting/) was held **February 18–22, 2023** in **San Diego, California**. I gave a **contributed talk** (not a poster) titled **“Local Alignment for Water Sites (LAWS) – describing allosteric water networks in enzymes”** (University of Toronto). I received a **Biophysical Society travel grant** to attend.

More information: [BPS 2023 general information](https://www.biophysics.org/2023meeting/general-info).
