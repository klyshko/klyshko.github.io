[WATOC 2020/2022](https://www.watoc.net/) (the 12th triennial congress) was held **July 3–8, 2022** at the **Vancouver Convention Centre**, following postponements from the original 2020 schedule. [IUPAC event notice](https://iupac.org/event/12th-triennial-congress-of-the-world-association-of-theoretical-and-computational-chemists-2/).

I presented a poster: **Equilibrium dynamics in protein crystals**—molecular dynamics and comparison with time-resolved structural experiments (building on our pump–probe/EF-X-style questions).
